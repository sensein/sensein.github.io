Collaborators

Working with others

Our work is open, our policies are explicit, and our collaboration network spans many institutions.

Our collaboration policies

  • Authorship is a function of intellectual contribution and we try to minimize author bloat. For multi-institution student collaborations, we identify ahead of time who the primary advisers and collaborators are on a project.
  • Our work is open. However, there have been prior situations where we have applied for patents during collaborations.
  • We tend to share any data we collect and any code we produce under open licenses (data: PDDL, code: Apache 2.0, doc: CC-BY).
  • Any digital research work we produce together should be re-executable and hopefully reproducible / generalizable.
